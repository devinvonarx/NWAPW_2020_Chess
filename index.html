Chess Played on a Server

This is a project for NWAPW to allow to people on different computers to play a chess game against each other.

Two can play chess through their browser with each other as long as they have an internet connection. One player will be white and move first, and when they make their move, the other player's board will update and the new player will be able to make a move. A simple login/register system is put into place to easily identify who is who, and can allow continuation of the game if a player accidently disconnects. A matchmaking system/queue is also put into place to pair people up. All rules of chess are implemented, including castling and en passant.
